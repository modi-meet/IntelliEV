Our Core Pillars

We've built our network on three pillars designed to address every aspect of the modern EV experience.

Emergency Response

In a collision, your vehicle automatically alerts emergency services with your precise location and vehicle data. Nearby IntelliEV users are also notified to offer immediate assistance.

Intelligent Connectivity

Using V2X (Vehicle-to-Everything) technology, your car communicates with others to report hazards, traffic jams, and road conditions in real-time, creating a collaborative and safer road network.

Smart Infrastructure

End range anxiety. Find and reserve available charging stations, get live updates on their status, and let your vehicle's navigation route you to the most efficient charging spot on your journey.

A Network for Everyone

IntelliEV is designed to benefit every stakeholder in the urban mobility ecosystem.

For EV Drivers

  • Drive with enhanced safety and peace of mind.
  • Receive real-time hazard and traffic alerts.
  • Easily find and access charging stations.
  • Be a part of a helpful driver community.

For Emergency Services

  • Receive instant, data-rich crash notifications.
  • Get precise GPS locations and vehicle diagnostics.
  • Reduce response times and save lives.
  • Coordinate with other responders in real-time.

For City Planners

  • Gather anonymous data on traffic flow.
  • Identify accident-prone areas for improvement.
  • Monitor charging infrastructure usage.
  • Build a smarter, more efficient city.
